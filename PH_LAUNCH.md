## First Comment (Founder Story)
Hey everyone — I built ChurnGuard because I was losing money and didn't even know it.

I run a handful of SaaS products on Stripe. One day I checked my dashboard and realized ~9% of my subscription revenue was disappearing to failed payments — expired cards, bank declines, network errors. Stripe retries, sure, but it's bare-bones. No emails. No SMS. No sequence. No follow-up.

So I built the thing I needed.

ChurnGuard sits on top of Stripe and does what Stripe doesn't — actually reaches out to customers when their payment fails. Smart multi-channel sequences (email + SMS), timing optimized for recovery, and a real dashboard showing you exactly how much revenue you're getting back.

The numbers:
- 47% industry average recovery rate
- 73% average with ChurnGuard
- Most customers update their card within 48 hours of the first email

Three plans — $49/$99/$249 based on your MRR. 14-day free trial, no card required.

This is fully self-serve. Connect Stripe, set your sequence, let it run. I built it for founders who don't want to talk to anyone — just want their money back.

Happy to answer questions. Would love your feedback.

— Scy
